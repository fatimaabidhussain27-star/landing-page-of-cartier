Cartier Heritage

For over 170 years, Cartier has been a symbol of luxury and refinement. Founded in Paris in 1847 by Louis-François Cartier, the Maison has established itself as the "Jeweler of Kings and the King of Jewelers."

Our ladies' watches reflect timeless design, elegance, and innovation, crafted for women who appreciate sophistication and enduring style.

Each Cartier creation tells a story of excellence, from the iconic Tank watch inspired by military vehicles to the graceful Ballon Bleu with its distinctive sapphire cabochon crown.
